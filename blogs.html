Achievement

Since its establishment, Cohee has grown into one of the most popular cafés in the city. With its minimalist and cozy interior concept, Cohee has successfully captured the attention of coffee enthusiasts and creative communities. In its first year of operation, Cohee was awarded "Best New Café" by a local culinary magazine. Additionally, Cohee managed to boost its sales by 150% within two years through effective digital marketing strategies. Their signature menu items, such as the Signature Cold Brew and Matcha Latte, have won several taste awards from coffee communities. Cohee also regularly hosts coffee workshops to educate customers about the best brewing techniques. Moreover, Cohee actively participates in various social events, including charity bazaars and city greening programs. Thanks to its consistent customer service, Cohee has earned a 4.9-star rating on Google Reviews with thousands of positive comments.
